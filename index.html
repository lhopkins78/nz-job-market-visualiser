Understanding occupations at risk of automation in the New Zealand economy. Updated March 2026. Based on Stats NZ Labour Market Statistics.

What You're Looking At

This is a research tool that visualizes occupations across the New Zealand job market. Each rectangle represents one occupation. The size of each rectangle shows how many people work in that role. The color shows the metric you select—toggle between AI exposure, median wage, and growth outlook to explore different dimensions.

How to Use It

  • Hover over any rectangle to see wages, employment numbers, and AI exposure reasoning.
  • Search to find your occupation or explore a sector.
  • Color by AI Exposure to see which jobs AI will reshape most.
  • Sort by employment size, wage, or AI exposure.

About the Data

Employment figures: Stats NZ Labour Market Statistics (December 2024 Quarter). Wages: Median weekly earnings by occupation. Growth outlook: Strong, moderate, stable, or declining demand.

AI exposure scores: Estimated using Claude 3.5 Sonnet, adapted from Andrej Karpathy's Jobs project. Based on how digital, routine, and automatable each role's core tasks are. This is not a prediction of job disappearance—it shows where AI tools will reshape work. Demand elasticity, new tools, and human choice matter enormously.
