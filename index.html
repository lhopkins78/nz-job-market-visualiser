NZ Job Market Visualiser

Understanding occupations, employment, and AI exposure in New Zealand

What you're looking at: This is a research tool that visualizes occupations across the New Zealand job market. Each rectangle's area represents total employment in that occupation. The color indicates a metric you select—toggle between AI exposure, median wage, and growth outlook to explore different dimensions of the job market.

Why this matters: AI is reshaping work. Some occupations face significant exposure to AI tools that can automate or augment key tasks. Others have natural barriers—physical presence, hands-on skill, real-time human judgment. This visualization helps you understand where your occupation sits.

How to use it:

  • Color by: Choose AI Exposure to see which jobs AI will reshape most.
  • Sort by: Reorder by employment size, wage level, AI exposure, or alphabetically.
  • Search: Find your occupation or explore a sector.
  • Hover: See wages, employment numbers, and AI exposure reasoning.

How to read this visualization:
Each rectangle represents an occupation. The size shows employment. The color indicates your selected metric. Hover over any rectangle to see detailed employment figures, wages, AI exposure scores, and reasoning about how AI will affect that role.
A note on AI Exposure scores: These are estimates based on occupation descriptions, not predictions of job disappearance. A high score means AI tools will reshape the work, not that jobs will vanish. Demand elasticity, new tools, and human choice matter enormously. Many high-exposure roles will transform and grow as workers become more productive with AI.
